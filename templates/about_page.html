About us

Our goal is to promote diversity and inclusion in society.
We strongly believe the first step to do this is by getting someone's name right. This app will help you to identify how a person's name is pronounced correctly.

Nomi encourages you to celebrate diversity by doing this in an educational and accessible way, with the help of audio recordings and braille alongside information about name origins, meanings and heritage.
